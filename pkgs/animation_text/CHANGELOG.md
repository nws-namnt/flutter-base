## 1.0.0

* Initial internal release.
* Text animation effects: Spring, ChimeBell, Scale, Rotate, Blur, Offset/Slide,
  Scramble, Reveal (sliding cursor), and Gravity (2D rigid-body physics via
  `forge2d`).
* Letter-by-letter and word-by-word animation with configurable duration,
  curves and modes (forward, reverse, repeat, repeat-with-reverse).
* External control via `AnimatedTextController` (play, pause, repeat, etc.).
* Packaged as a pure Dart/Flutter library (no native platform code).
